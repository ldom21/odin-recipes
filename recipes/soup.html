Potato and Kale Soup

Description

Hereâ€™s a simple and delicious kale and potato soup recipe, perfect for winter days!


Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 large onion
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 4-5 medium potatoes
  • 6 cups vegetable or chicken broth
  • 1 bunch kale
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Prepare the ingredients:
    • Chop the onion and garlic
    • Peel and cube the potatoes
    • Remove the stems from the kale and chop the leaves
  2. Cook the Onion and Garlic:
    • Heat the olive oil in a large pot over medium heat
    • Add the chopped onion and cook until translucent, about 5 minutes
    • Add the minced garlic and cook for another 1-2 minutes, until fragrant
  3. Add the Potatoes and Broth:
    • Add the cubed potatoes to the pot
    • Pour in the vegetable or chicken broth
    • Add the thyme and rosemary
    • Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer until the potatoes are tender, about 15-20 minutes
  4. Add the Kale:
    • Stir in the chopped kale and continue to simmer for another 5-10 minutes, until the kale is tender
  5. Season and Serve:
    • Taste the soup and season with salt and pepper to taste
    • Serve hot, with crusty bread on the side if desired
